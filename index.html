Welcome to Rudra Pet Clinic

Welcome to Rudra Pet Clinic, your trusted destination for exceptional veterinary care. At Rudra pets, we believe your pets are family, and they deserve the highest standard of love, care, and medical attention. Our team of experienced veterinarians and animal care experts are dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services—from routine vaccinations and general checkups to emergency care, diagnostics, surgeries, and wellness treatments.

We are available 24/7 for any pet-related emergencies, ensuring your furry companions receive immediate attention when they need it most.

We offer a wide range of services including blood tests, urine analysis, fluid therapy, ultrasound (coming soon), ECG and BP monitoring, and inpatient facilities. We also provide pet products such as food, supplements, grooming items, and accessories. Our mission is to treat every pet with compassion, and ensure their health and happiness through expert care and modern facilities.

At Rudra Pet Clinic, we don’t just treat pets—we care for them like our own. Visit us today and experience veterinary care where quality, compassion, and trust meet.
