About Us

At Rudra Pet Clinic, we understand that pets are not just animals – they are family. That’s why our mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality veterinary care to ensure the health and happiness of your beloved companions. Located in the heart of India, we are proud to offer a comprehensive range of services designed to cater to the specific needs of pets of all breeds, ages, and sizes. From routine wellness check-ups to complex surgeries, our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, and our team of skilled veterinarians is dedicated to delivering exceptional medical care. We are committed to offering not just treatments but a personalized, holistic approach to your pet’s well-being, focusing on both their physical and emotional health.

We are available 24/7 for any pet-related emergencies. Our services include preventive care, which is the cornerstone of maintaining long-term health. We provide regular health check-ups, vaccinations, parasite control, and nutritional counseling to ensure that your pets are healthy and happy for years to come. For pets requiring advanced care, we offer comprehensive diagnostics using modern tools to accurately diagnose and treat a wide range of illnesses. Whether it’s surgery, dental care, or specialized treatments, we take great pride in using the latest medical techniques and ensuring that your pet is as comfortable as possible throughout their treatment journey. We believe that prevention is better than cure, which is why we emphasize educating pet owners on the importance of regular veterinary visits and proactive health management. At Rudra Pet Clinic, we treat every pet with the same love and attention we would give our own, and we are committed to building lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, care, and the highest standard of service.

We invite you to visit us and experience the Rudra Pet Clinic difference. Your pet’s health is our priority, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

Meet Our Core Team

Dr. V. M. Dhoot

Dr. V. M. Dhoot is a highly experienced veterinary professional with over 30+ years of expertise in Veterinary Medicine. He completed his undergraduate studies in 1991, followed by his post-graduation in 1993, and a post-doctoral degree in 2012. His passion for academics and research is evident through his remarkable contributions, including 120 research publications, 16 technical bulletins, and 25 popular articles. Dr. Dhoot has mentored numerous students, guiding nine as a Major Advisor, nineteen as a Minor Advisor, and one post-doctoral student.

Dr. Varad Dhoot

Dr. Varad Dhoot is a dedicated veterinary professional with 5+ years of extensive experience in companion animal and exotic animal practice. He completed his Bachelor's degree from Nagpur Veterinary College and later pursued his Master's in Medicine from the same institution. Dr. Dhoot is deeply passionate about clinical veterinary medicine, with specialized expertise in diagnosing and treating complex health issues across a wide range of species.

Dr. Devkishan

Dr. Devkishan is a dedicated veterinary professional with 3+ years of experience in clinical veterinary practices. He completed his Bachelor's degree from Nagpur Veterinary College and his Master's degree from the College of Veterinary and Animal Sciences, Bikaner. With a strong passion for animal health and welfare, Dr. Devkishan is committed to providing high-quality care through accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and preventive health strategies.
